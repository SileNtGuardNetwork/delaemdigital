## 10. Legal and Risk

Questions:

1. Does the project process personal data?
2. Is the niche regulated?
3. Are there medical, finance, education, investment, real estate, or legal claims?
4. Are licenses, certificates, or disclaimers required?
5. What claims must not be used?
6. What consent text is required?
7. Where should data be stored?

Required minimum:

- privacy policy
- personal data consent
- cookie notice if analytics or cookies are used
- consent checkbox near forms
- no pre-checked consent
- legal links in footer and near forms
